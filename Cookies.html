Fried Oreos are a beloved indulgent treat that take the classic Oreo cookie and elevate it to a whole new level of flavor! The process of making fried Oreos starts with the Oreo cookie: two chocolate wafer cookies with a sweet, creamy filling in the middle. The cookies are then dipped in a smooth, thick mixture of Pancake Mix and eggs, that is slightly sweet and slightly savory, creating a delicious combination with the Oreo's crunch. Then, the mixture is deep fries in hot oil.

Enjoy its sweet, creamy, and crispy taste that is so HEAVENLY DELICIOUS!!!
